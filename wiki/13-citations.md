# 13 · Citations

If you use Baryon in research, education or art, please cite both the
tool **and** the underlying data and methods.

## Tool

> Gülay, S. (2026). *Baryon: A Galaxy Inspector & Sonification Tool*
> (v1). Source code and wiki, this repository.

A `CITATION.cff` file is included for automatic resolution by Zenodo,
GitHub, and reference managers.

## Data

- **SDSS DR8.** Aihara, H., Allende Prieto, C., An, D., et al. (2011).
  *The Eighth Data Release of the Sloan Digital Sky Survey.* ApJS,
  **193**, 29. doi:10.1088/0067-0049/193/2/29
- **MPA-JHU value-added catalogue.**
  - Kauffmann, G., Heckman, T. M., White, S. D. M., et al. (2003).
    *Stellar masses and star formation histories for 10⁵ galaxies from
    the SDSS.* MNRAS, **341**, 33.
  - Brinchmann, J., Charlot, S., White, S. D. M., et al. (2004).
    *The physical properties of star-forming galaxies in the
    low-redshift Universe.* MNRAS, **351**, 1151.
  - Tremonti, C. A., Heckman, T. M., Kauffmann, G., et al. (2004).
    *The origin of the mass–metallicity relation.* ApJ, **613**, 898.
  - Salim, S., Rich, R. M., Charlot, S., et al. (2007).
    *UV star formation rates in the local universe.* ApJS, **173**, 267.
- **Galactic extinction.** Schlegel, D. J., Finkbeiner, D. P., &
  Davis, M. (1998). *Maps of dust infrared emission for use in
  estimation of reddening and CMB radiation foregrounds.* ApJ, **500**, 525.

## Methods used in the renderer

- **Sérsic profile.** Sérsic, J. L. (1963). *Influence of the
  atmospheric and instrumental dispersion on the brightness
  distribution in a galaxy.* BAAA, **6**, 41.
- **Sérsic b_n approximation.** Ciotti, L., & Bertin, G. (1999).
  *Analytical properties of the R^(1/m) law.* A&A, **352**, 447.
- **asinh colour stretch.** Lupton, R., Blanton, M. R., Fekete, G., et al.
  (2004). *Preparing red–green–blue images from CCD data.*
  PASP, **116**, 133.

## Methods used in the synth

- **Web Audio API.** W3C Recommendation, current edition.
- **Loudness.** ITU-R Recommendation BS.1770-4 (2015). *Algorithms
  to measure audio programme loudness and true-peak audio level.*

## Methods used in the LR module

- **Ridge regression.** Hoerl, A. E., & Kennard, R. W. (1970).
  *Ridge regression: biased estimation for nonorthogonal problems.*
  Technometrics, **12**, 55.

## Methods used in the route engine

- **Dijkstra's algorithm.** Dijkstra, E. W. (1959). *A note on two
  problems in connexion with graphs.* Numer. Math., **1**, 269.
